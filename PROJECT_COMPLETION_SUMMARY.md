## 🎯 OPTIMIZATION STRATEGY

### Priority Ranking System
The optimized dataset uses a smart priority ranking to select the best email for each domain:

**Tier 1 (Highest Priority - Support/Help):**
1. `support@` - Direct support contact
2. `help@` - Help/support contact
3. `contact@` - General contact
4. `info@` - Information contact

**Tier 2 (Business Contact):**
5. `hello@` - Friendly business contact
6. `business@` - Business inquiries
7. `inquiry@` - General inquiries
8. `sales@` - Sales contact (LOWEST PRIORITY)

**Additional Patterns:**
